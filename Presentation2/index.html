STAR Stories

  • Situation: During my time as a customer service representative, I received a call from an upset customer who had received the wrong order.
    Task: My task was to resolve the issue and ensure that the customer was satisfied with the resolution.
    Action: I listened attentively to the customer's concerns, apologized for the inconvenience, and offered to replace the incorrect item at no cost. I also offered to expedite the shipping to ensure the customer received the correct order as soon as possible.
    Result: The customer was grateful for my prompt and courteous handling of the situation and left a positive review on the company's website. The company also recognized my efforts and awarded me with an Employee of the Month title.
  • Situation: While working on a group project for my Java class, our team was faced with a tight deadline and conflicting ideas on how to approach the project.
    Task: I facilitated a group discussion to clarify our goals and assigned tasks to each team member based on their strengths.
    Action: I also set up regular check-ins to ensure everyone was making progress and offered support when necessary.
    Result: We were able to submit a high-quality project on time, and we received an A grade for the assignment.
  • Situation: During my internship at a friends company, I was given the task of improving the company's social media presence.
    Task: I conducted research on the best practices for social media marketing, created a content calendar, and wrote engaging posts for various social media platforms.
    Action: I also analyzed the engagement metrics and made adjustments to the social media strategy accordingly.
    Result: As a result of my efforts, the company's social media engagement increased by 50% and we gained 1,000 new followers in just three months.
